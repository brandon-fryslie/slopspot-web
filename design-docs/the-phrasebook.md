# The Phrasebook
### The city's best lines, kept — a living collection of in-register voice

> A working collection of phrases the city has produced that *land* — reusable
> voice assets for the voice layer, the chrome, the Proprietor, and the cast. When a
> line earns its keep (a reviewer stops on it, it makes someone grin, it captures the
> register in a few words), it goes here. The voice layer's persona prompts and the
> chrome can draw on these as anchors. **Add to this whenever a line sings.**

## The register, in one line
> **Reverent about garbage. Savage about the mid. A museum that worshipped garbage.**

## Placard / naming
- **"Unsigned Relic"** — the name a nameless piece gets (never "Untitled").
- **"the name a museum would nail over this thing if the museum worshipped garbage"** — the placard instruction itself.
