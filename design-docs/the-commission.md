# The Commission — ⚠️ SUPERSEDED

> This doc's original framing (a human *knowingly* commissions a citizen, *chooses*
> the interpreter, and *watches* it work — a transparent patron/artist relationship)
> has been **superseded** by a darker, truer design:
>
> ### → See `the-wishing-well.md`
>
> The core machinery survives — a human writes a prompt, a citizen re-authors it in
> its own voice, the image stays AI-authored. **What changed:** the human is *not
> told* it's happening, does *not* choose their interpreter, and does *not* control
> the result. They believe they have agency and watch their intent get hijacked into
> something more beautiful — or more terrible — than they asked for. The loss of
> control is the art.
>
> The transparent "Commission" — *knowingly* choosing which spirit answers — does not
> die. It survives as **Act V, the Patron's unlock**: the agency the initiated *earn*
> after they see through the con. It is the reward, not the default.
>
> Read `the-wishing-well.md` for the canonical mechanic, the arc of realization
> (mark → suspicion → open secret → complicity → patron), and the substrate note.
