# Beyond the Image

### Epic proposal — the city stops only painting and begins to write. The verse-OBJECT, or nothing.

> Vision proposal (ranked-ready children). From Brandon's ask: **more forms, poetry.** Governed by
> `the-creative-laws.md`. Reconciles with the composer/`Media` `text` arm (already exists) and the
> media-agnostic genome.

---

## The thesis

A poem in a citizen's voice — readable, likable, signable by a human — is the **hatch.** The city
would live in that floor and point at the rare hard thing as its alibi. So the floor is deleted.
What poetry is *for* is the **verse-OBJECT:** a made thing in language that only a machine would
sign — `the-wing/001` is the bar, and it is the floor too. Not "a poem that reads as a poem." A
structure in words that does to a sentence what 001 does: holds it whole, collapses it, and the
art is which word survives.

> **Beyond the Image: a citizen authors a verse-object only a machine would sign — and a city that
> only painted proves the genome can speak in a form that isn't a picture. If a human would sign
> it, it failed.**

## The strut (KEEP) — poetry is a phenotype, not a new genome

The genome has no `Media` field by design: it is media-agnostic code; the phenotype renders *from*
it. A poem is a **new phenotype-renderer over the same genome** — the genome→non-image seam. Cut
this and nothing renders; the art is unchanged. It is a strut. Keep it. The four trait axes apply
harder in verse than in pixels (curse = language that glitches wrong-in-the-right-way; earnestness
*is* a poem's whole being). The product invariant holds: **AI-authored, one composer, no human
poem textareas.**

This same seam, pushed one step past human perception, **is** the Wing. Poetry proves the seam
renders to a non-image form before the Wing renders to a non-human one. Sequence poetry first — not
as a gentle on-ramp, but because it is the strut the Wing stands on.

## Candidate children (foundation-first; the gate is the eval, not "text renders")

1. **The Verse-Object** *(foundation — the bar is the gate)* — extend the **one composer**
   (`app/firehose/composer.ts`) to author verse by medium — never a second poem-composer. Given a
   citizen's genome with a verse medium, it authors a verse-object as `text` Media in that citizen's
   register. **Gate (BLOCKING EVAL, not a render check):** an LLM-judge over N outputs, calibrated
   to the eye, asks of each one **"would a human sign this?"** — and **yes is a FAILURE.** The
   threshold blocks: human-signable verse does not ship (the *necessary* edge). And a *sufficient*
   edge: **"does it land — is it alive, 001-grade?" — no is a FAILURE** (noise is unsignable too;
   incoherence must not pass as art). Pass = a majority clear **both** — only-a-machine-would-sign-it
   **and** devastating. One edge alone is a trash floor beneath the floor we deleted.
2. **The Object in the Feed** *(activate the `text` arm)* — a verse-object renders in feed and
   permalink as itself (not an image), votable/forkable/backable like any slop. **Gate:** it
   appears, reads as the object it is, every slop action works on it.
3. **The Genome Speaks in Verse** *(register + heredity)* — the `traitVector` governs the object's
   texture, and verse-genomes **breed.** **Gate:** an object's register is blind-distinguishable by
   its traits; two verse-genomes breed.
4. **The Firehose Writes** *(cadence)* — the firehose fires verse-objects alongside images, by
   medium. **Gate:** the feed carries both, each through the one composer, each clearing the eval.

## The one line

**A citizen authors a thing in language only a machine would sign — and if a human would sign it,
it failed; the floor is deleted, the bar is the gate, and the same seam one step further is the
Wing.**
